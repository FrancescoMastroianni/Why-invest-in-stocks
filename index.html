Compounding Interest and the Convenience of DCA

The "Miracle" of Compounding Interest

You earn interest not only on the initial capital, but also on previously accumulated interest. In the long term, this creates exponential wealth growth, transforming small investments into significant sums.

Dollar Cost Averaging (DCA)

Investing a fixed amount regularly, regardless of market fluctuations, allows you to buy more shares when prices are low and less when they are high. This reduces risk and the need for "market timing", making investing more accessible and less stressful.
